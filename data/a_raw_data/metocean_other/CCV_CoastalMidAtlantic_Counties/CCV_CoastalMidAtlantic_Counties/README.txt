May 2018


These GIS data and map symbology were generated in support of the report,

'Climate Change Vulnerabilities in the Coastal Mid-Atlantic Region. CA: 

The Mid-Atlantic Regional Council on the Ocean, 2018.' 

(Colgan, Charles S., Juliano Calil, Hauke Kite-Powell, Di Jin, Porter Hoagland,

Shaun R. Richards, Clesi Bennet, and Emma Ross)



This report was prepared jointly by the Center for the Blue Economy of the

Middlebury Institute of International Studies at Monterey and the Marine Policy

Center of the Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution



The project was a collaboration of the Mid-Atlantic Regional Council on the Ocean (MARCO)

and the Monmouth University Urban Coast Institute (UCI), made possible by a grant from

the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA). 




To learn more about how this data was used please read the full report at:
http://midatlanticocean.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/Climate-Change-Vulnerabilities-in-the-Coastal-Mid-Atlantic-Region.pdf




Contents:

1. GIS Data: CCV_CoastalMidAtlantic_Counties.gdb

- Format: ArcGIS Geodatabase 10.x

- Projection/Coordinate System: WGS 1984 Web Mercator (auxiliary sphere)*

*NOTE: These data are prepared in Web Mercator projection/coordinate system

for the purposes of web services visualization on the MARCO Portal.  



2. Metadata: \Metadata\*.html


3. Map Symbology: \Report_Maps_Symbology\*.lyr

- ArcGIS layer files by report map name and figure number

- Full report in PDF format also available in this folder.
